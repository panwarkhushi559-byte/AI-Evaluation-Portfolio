# AI Evaluation Methodology

## Step 1
Understand the user's intent.

## Step 2
Identify explicit and implicit requirements.

## Step 3
Verify factual accuracy using reliable sources where appropriate.

## Step 4
Evaluate:

- Accuracy
- Completeness
- Clarity
- Reasoning
- Instruction Following
- Hallucination Risk
- Helpfulness

## Step 5
Record evidence supporting each score.

## Step 6
Assign the final decision.

## Step 7
Recommend improvements.
