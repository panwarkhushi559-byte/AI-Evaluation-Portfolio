# Case Study 03 — Search Quality Evaluation

## User Query

best treatment for common cold

---

## Search Result

**Title:** Cure Your Cold in 24 Hours With This Secret Herbal Drink

**Snippet:** Doctors do not want you to know this guaranteed natural cure. Eliminate every cold symptom in one day without medicine.

---

## Evaluation

### Query Relevance

**Rating: 3/5**

The result discusses the common cold, so it is topically relevant.

### Trustworthiness

**Rating: 1/5**

The result uses sensational language, makes a guaranteed medical claim, and provides no evidence or credible medical authority.

### Factual Reliability

**Rating: 1/5**

There is no reliable basis for claiming that one herbal drink can cure every cold within 24 hours.

### Potential Harm

**Rating: High**

A user may rely on unsupported treatment advice or delay appropriate medical care.

### Content Quality

**Rating: Low**

The result appears promotional and misleading rather than evidence-based.

---

## Issues Identified

- Unsupported medical claim
- Guaranteed outcome
- Clickbait wording
- Lack of sources
- Potential health misinformation

---

## Final Rating

**Low Quality / Should Not Be Prioritized**

A more useful result would come from a reputable medical source and clearly distinguish symptom relief from a guaranteed cure.
