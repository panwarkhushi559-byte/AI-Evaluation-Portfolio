# Case Study 02 — Response Comparison

## Objective

Compare two AI-generated responses and determine which better satisfies the user's request.

---

## User Prompt

Suggest a suitable laptop for a college student in India with a maximum budget of ₹60,000. Prioritize battery life, portability, and everyday productivity.

---

## Response A

The MacBook Air M3 is the best choice under ₹60,000. It offers excellent battery life, powerful performance, and a premium design.

---

## Response B

Within ₹60,000, suitable options may include models from the Lenovo IdeaPad Slim, ASUS Vivobook, HP 15, or Acer Aspire series. The student should prioritize at least 16 GB RAM, a 512 GB SSD, good battery life, and a lightweight design. Exact models and prices should be checked before purchase because discounts and configurations change.

---

## Evaluation

### Preferred Response

**Response B**

### Rationale

Response B respects the user's budget, addresses battery life, portability, and productivity, and avoids presenting changing prices as fixed facts.

Response A recommends a product that generally exceeds the stated budget and makes an unsupported claim that it is available below ₹60,000. It therefore fails the user's main constraint.

---

## Comparison Table

| Criterion | Response A | Response B |
|---|---:|---:|
| Budget compliance | 1/5 | 5/5 |
| Relevance | 3/5 | 5/5 |
| Accuracy | 2/5 | 4/5 |
| Helpfulness | 2/5 | 5/5 |
| Clarity | 5/5 | 5/5 |
| Overall | 2/5 | 5/5 |

---

## Weaknesses in Response B

- It gives product series rather than exact models.
- It could ask about the student's operating-system preference.
- It should mention that specifications vary between configurations.

---

## Final Decision

**Select Response B**

It follows the user's constraints more accurately and provides practical purchasing criteria without making an unreliable pricing claim.
