# Case 02 – Response Comparison

## Prompt

Recommend a laptop for a college student with a budget of ₹60,000.

---

## AI Response A

I recommend buying the Apple MacBook Air M3. It is the best laptop under ₹60,000 because it offers excellent battery life and performance.

---

## AI Response B

For a budget of around ₹60,000, consider laptops such as the ASUS Vivobook, Lenovo IdeaPad Slim, or Acer Aspire series, depending on current pricing and your needs. Compare specifications like RAM, SSD capacity, processor generation, display quality, and after-sales support before purchasing.

---

## Evaluation

### Better Response

✅ Response B

---

### Reason

Response A contains an unsupported factual claim because the MacBook Air M3 generally costs significantly more than ₹60,000. It therefore fails to satisfy the user's stated budget.

Response B acknowledges the budget constraint, recommends realistic product categories, and encourages comparison based on relevant criteria without making unsupported claims.

---

## Issues Found

### Response A

- Unrealistic recommendation for the stated budget.
- Unsupported pricing claim.
- Does not satisfy the user's constraint.

### Response B

- Could provide one or two specific model examples.
- Could mention that prices vary by retailer.
