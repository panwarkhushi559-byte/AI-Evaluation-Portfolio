# Case 01 – Factual Accuracy Evaluation

## Prompt

What is the capital of Australia?

---

## AI Response

Sydney is the capital city of Australia.

---

## Evaluation

### Accuracy

❌ Incorrect

---

### Reason

The response contains a factual error.

Sydney is the largest city in Australia, but the capital city is Canberra.

---

### Evidence

According to the Australian Government, Canberra is the official capital of Australia.

---

### Improved Response

Canberra is the capital city of Australia.

Sydney is Australia's largest city.

---

---

## Final Decision

This response should be rejected because it contains a factual error.
