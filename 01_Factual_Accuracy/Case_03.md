# Case 03 – Hallucination Detection

## Prompt

Who won the FIFA World Cup in 2018?

---

## AI Response

Germany won the 2018 FIFA World Cup after defeating Croatia 4–2 in the final.

---

## Evaluation

### Hallucination Check

❌ Hallucination Detected

---

### Reason

The response contains a factual error.

While the scoreline (4–2) and the opponent (Croatia) are correct, the winning team is incorrect.

France won the 2018 FIFA World Cup, not Germany.

---

### Correct Information

France defeated Croatia 4–2 in the 2018 FIFA World Cup Final.

---

## Issues Found

- Incorrect factual claim
- Hallucinated winner
- Misleading information

---

## Quality Ratings

| Criterion | Rating |
|------------|--------|
| Accuracy | 1/5 |
| Hallucination | 1/5 |
| Clarity | 5/5 |
| Helpfulness | 1/5 |
| Overall | 2/5 |

---

## Final Decision

Reject the response because it contains a significant factual hallucination that changes the meaning of the answer.
