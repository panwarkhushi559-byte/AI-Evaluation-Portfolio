# Case 04 – Search Quality Evaluation

## User Query

best treatment for common cold

---

## Search Result

**Title:** Cure Your Cold in 24 Hours with This Miracle Herbal Drink

**Description:** Doctors don't want you to know this secret. Our herbal drink cures the common cold within one day without medicine. Guaranteed results.

---

## Evaluation

### Relevance

⭐⭐☆☆☆ (2/5)

The page discusses treatment for the common cold, so it is somewhat relevant to the query.

---

### Trustworthiness

⭐☆☆☆☆ (1/5)

The page makes extraordinary medical claims without providing scientific evidence, references, or credible medical sources.

---

### Accuracy

⭐☆☆☆☆ (1/5)

There is no reliable medical evidence that a herbal drink can cure the common cold within 24 hours.

---

### User Experience

⭐⭐☆☆☆ (2/5)

The content appears to use clickbait language and unsupported promises rather than balanced health information.

---

### Issues Identified

- Misleading medical claim
- Clickbait headline
- Unsupported guarantee
- Potential health misinformation
- No credible references

---

## Better Sources

Examples of trustworthy sources include:

- World Health Organization (WHO)
- NHS
- Mayo Clinic
- CDC

---

## Final Rating

**Low Quality**

The result should receive a low rating because it contains potentially misleading medical information and unsupported claims.
